The Urban Farmhand works with you to make your garden dreams come true, and your urban farmyard fantasies a reality. We provide services such as garden installation and maintenance, chicken husbandry, fence installation, compost system design, raised bed construction, and more! We currently serve Duluth as well as the north and south shores of Lake Superior.

I come from a long line of dairy farmers, and spent my early years milking cows and growing raspberries on my grandparents' farm in central Wisconsin. Farming, and a love for the land, runs in my blood. I studied plant ecology, wetland plant restoration, and forest management in college, and went on to study sustainability in grad school. I bring a broad background of big-picture sustainabiltiy to my work, together with a love for all things green feathery, furry, and muddy. Coupled with extensive greenhouse propagation and landscape installation experience, I am ready and able to turn your backyard into a Northern native woodland, or a beautiful, hyper local food production system! backyard (or front yard!)
